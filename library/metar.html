METAR
Synonym weather briefings
Type Meteorology, Human Factors
Definition A codified weather information requested by a pilot during flight that has a specific structure.

Meteorological Aerodrome Report (METAR) is a codified weather information that is automatically generated by weather stations installed at airports or forecasted by a dedicated meteorologist. The weather information has a specific structure (like grammar). For example, imagine there is an airport whose codename is ABCD and a pilot requested weather info on August 24th 2023:

METAR ABCD 242153Z 17010G20KT 15SM BKN029 OVC160 26/21 A2984

The above example is decoded as follows:

  • Location: ABCD Airport
  • Date: August 24th 2023, 2153 UTC
  • Wind: 170 True at 10 knots gusting to 20 knots
  • Visibility: 15 statute miles
  • Clouds: Broken clouds at 2900 feet, Overcast 16000 feet
  • Temperature / Dewpoint: 26 and 21 degrees Celsius
  • Altimeter: 29.84
