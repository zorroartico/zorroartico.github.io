This article belongs to the Aeronautics category.

Fatigue
Synonym Red-eye
Type Human Factors
Definition Being tired, exhausted, or sleepy during a flight operation.

Fatigue should not be overlooked as a minor symptom of a long-haul flight. It can happen on a short-haul flight too. Fatigue happens due to lack of sleep prior to flight and imbalance in flight schedule. Combatting fatigue is impossible because the only solution is to get plenty of sleep. Sleep cycle is also mathematically modelled in order to learn more about an individual's wakefulness history.

As mentioned in the aviation safety article, travellers might have a sharp knowledge on an airline's safety records. Although there is no "one size fits all" type of solution to fatigue management, it might be a good idea to keep a sleep journal and ensure that sleep schedule so far remains uninterrupted. Fatigue causes spatial disorientation, loss of situation awareness, and losing focus and motor ability.
