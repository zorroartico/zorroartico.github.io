Heat Wave

This article belongs to the Climate category.

Heat Wave
Synonym heat streak, heat events
Coverage Global
Cause Various blocking conditions.
Effects power shortages, strained health services, crop and livestock damages, health complications

Heat wave has been on the headlines of news articles these days. Heat wave is caused by several blocking conditions. One example is Omega Block, which occurs when an area of high pressure is sandwiched between two areas of low pressure to the left and right. Its shape is similar to that of Omega alphabet when viewed on a surface chart. It can bring in warmer and hotter weather (and clear skies) if the high pressure is located right above. The two cut off lows will be rainy and cloudy.

There is an ongoing discussion on how climate change can exacerbate the strength of heat waves. Heat waves have recently become more life-threatening and currently the scientific consensus is that heat wave will become more severe if climate change is ignored.
