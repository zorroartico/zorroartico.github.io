Ocean Heat Wave

This article belongs to the Climate category.

Ocean Heat Wave
Synonym Rising ocean surface temperature, Marine heatwave
Coverage Global
Cause Less winds from Sahara Desert, climate crisis [1]
Effects loss of marine species, extreme weather, coral bleaching, less absorption of heat produced from carbon dioxide

Ocean heat wave is not something to be ignored. If there is heat wave in the air (i.e., Omega Block), then there is ocean heat wave under the sea. Ocean absorbs a lot of excess heat and helps in maintaining stable climate [2]. Waves and tides' task is to re-distribute excess heat from warmer to cooler regions [2]. However, as ocean temperature rises, it is rather critical to look at how much ocean will keep that excess heat because it will, someday, return all that heat energy back to humans.

Ocean heat wave will threaten fisheries sector. Seafood will be very difficult to get from grocery stores as ocean heat wave worsens. Scarcity of natural resources have always led to political conflicts, and this seems likely over the coming years if ocean is neglected from new environmental policies. There is a need to keep the ocean clean and healthy.
